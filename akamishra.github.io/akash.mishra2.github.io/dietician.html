Laura Beth Klein, Well-known Dietitian/Nutritionist

Laura Klein, RDN is the registered dietitian for BPG Pulse Health & Fitness Programs and UC WorkStrong Program. She comes to us with a broad based background within the field of nutrition. As a nutrition expert, Laura provides scientifically sound health and nutrition advice presented in a practical and applicable manner. She has a passion for reaching people with nutrition advice to enable them to change eating habits with the final result of long term lifestyle changes. Laura’s background includes public speaking, media-related work, outpatient counseling and community nutrition. The specific areas of her expertise are individualized weight management, eating disordered nutrition counseling, heart healthy nutrition, diabetes and pre and post bariatric surgery nutrition counseling. Laura holds degrees in both nutrition and psychology as well as being certified in adult weight management.
