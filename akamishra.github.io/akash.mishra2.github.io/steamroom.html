STEAM BATH & STEAM SHOWER

Pure indulgence for body and soul: bathing in hot steam boosts your feeling of well-being and benefits your health in equal measure. Our steam baths and steam showers embody this effect impressively. Discover our world of steam baths! Steam baths date back to ancient times. Even the ancient Greeks and Romans indulged in the healthy and pleasant occupation of perspiring in steam baths. Steam baths are rather like saunas in their impact on the body. However, the temperature in a steam bath is lower, ranging from 43°-46°C, and with 100% humidity,
it is also considerably more humid. Steam bathing is healthy, and it’s also the perfect way to relax. The hot steam is particularly beneficial for people suffering from respiratory disorders or rheumatism. It makes depression and low spirits evaporate, leaving you feeling refreshed and fit. Fragrant aromas diffuse gently through the room with the steam, supporting the curative effect and positive mood of the steam bath. Those who love beautiful skin also swear by the humid warmth of steam baths. Here, pores are opened, flaky skin is shed and impurities simply disappear. The humid warmth boosts your circulation and cleanses and revives your skin. In short: your skin feels smoother, purer and silky soft.
